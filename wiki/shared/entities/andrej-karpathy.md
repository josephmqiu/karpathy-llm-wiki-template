# Andrej Karpathy

AI researcher and educator. Former director of AI at Tesla, co-founder of OpenAI.

## Relevance to this wiki

Author of the [[llm-wiki-pattern]], proposed in an April 2026 GitHub gist ([[karpathy-llm-wiki-gist]]). The gist describes using LLMs to incrementally build and maintain persistent markdown wikis as an alternative to RAG-based knowledge retrieval. This wiki is a direct instantiation of that pattern.

## Reported results

Applied the pattern to one research topic, growing it to ~100 articles and ~400K words without writing the prose himself ([[llm-wiki-research-synthesis]]).
